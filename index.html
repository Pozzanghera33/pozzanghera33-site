Our Vision

Hi, I am MASTASHIO, the founder and character-leader of POZZANGHERA 33.

"Pozzanghera" means Puddle in Italian. We believe in the pure, unbridled joy of a child jumping into a rain puddle with their favorite boots on. No rules, just splashes of imagination.

Based in Osaka, Japan, we create indie games that blend chaotic physics with a unique sense of humor.
